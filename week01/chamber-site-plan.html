Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

"Lindon Chamber of Commerce."

Site Purpose

The Lindon Chamber of commerce is meant to provide insight on the activities and business in Lindon, Utah. We give users and members an idea of what Lindon is as a community.

Site Scenarios

  • What events are happening in Lindon this month?
  • Where is Lindon located?
  • What are the current conditions in Lindon?
